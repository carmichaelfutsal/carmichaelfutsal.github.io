Coming in winter 2015, we will start our inaugural season of futsal in Carmichael California!

Futsal is a quick-paced, small-sided game played on a small field of play- in our case on a basketball court. It is a sport played by millions all over the world as it is fun to play and fun to watch! Futsal is recognized as a fantastic way to develop soccer skills.

Register

1. Coach: fill out the team registration form. 2. We will email further instructions about the process. 3. Players and coach must register with US Futsal for insurance. 4. Team fees are due before the first game.

Rules

Futsal rules are simple: similar to outdoor soccer, but instead of throw-ins when the ball goes out of play, it is returned in play with a kick. Futsal is low contact compared to other forms of soccer- it is about skills!
